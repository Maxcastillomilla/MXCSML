## Overview

DEX Pisos Modulares is a Chilean company specializing in high-resistance modular flooring systems for industrial and commercial applications. The project involved building a fully immersive virtual showroom and museum experience to communicate the complete lifecycle of their product — from raw material and manufacturing, through distribution and installation, to end use in demanding environments such as mining operations.

The virtual environment replaced traditional product catalogues and trade fair displays with an interactive 3D space accessible remotely, allowing clients to understand the product's scale, quality, and context without visiting physical sites.

## Experience Design

The showroom was structured as a navigable museum with distinct zones:

- **Manufacturing gallery** — 3D-modeled recreations of the production process, materials, and quality stages
- **Distribution and installation** — spatial sequences showing logistics, on-site assembly, and configuration options
- **End-use environments** — full-scale 3D scenes depicting the flooring in mining operations, industrial facilities, and commercial spaces with heavy machinery
- **360° video gallery** — immersive footage embedded within the virtual space for documentary context
- **Video gallery** — curated product and process videos accessible in-world

## Role & Tools

- Unreal Engine 5 — environment design, product visualization, gallery logic
- 3D modeling of manufacturing machinery, flooring systems, and deployment scenarios
- 360° video integration within the virtual environment
- Blueprint scripting for navigation and media playback
