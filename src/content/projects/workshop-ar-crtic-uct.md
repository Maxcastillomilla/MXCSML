## Overview

A one-week workshop on augmented reality and Instagram filter creation, developed at Universidad Católica de Temuco (UCT) in collaboration with CRT+IC — Centro para la Revolución Tecnológica en Industrias Creativas, a public-private organization that brings technological development closer to creative industries in Chile.

The workshop introduced participants to the tools and methodologies needed to design and deploy AR filters, covering the full pipeline from concept to a finished interactive experience. Beyond technical instruction, the program included supporting frameworks for structuring a creative project from scratch.

## Workshop Content

- Augmented reality fundamentals — how AR overlays interact with camera input and facial/surface tracking
- Filter creation tools — software and platforms for building Instagram/Facebook AR experiences
- Creative methodologies — structured approaches for developing a project concept within a short timeframe
- Project development — participants worked on individual or group projects throughout the week

## Outcome

The projects developed during the workshop were exhibited at the university at the end of the week, presenting the AR experiences to the wider academic community.

## Role

Workshop instructor / facilitator — designed and delivered the program in collaboration with CRT+IC.
